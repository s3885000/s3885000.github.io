Feedbacks

Overall, with the input, I received from two of my tutors. The range of views on the positives to the drawbacks, to name a couple. But in the end, they were involved in the idea that I was sharing with them. The functionality I described in the project is doable, but the most critical aspect for both tutors is how the user's private information is being kept. The project proposal will be added because I clarified to them regarding cybersecurity in and out of the IT Technologies section. And with Blockchain and Cryptocurrencies, it may be useful for the project in the future if the project is practical. The tutors have defined the main drawback of the project plan because older citizens may not benefit from this latest function due to lack of experience when it comes to utilizing technologies such as smartphones. Yet, most citizens in this digital era are slowly consuming technology information. So with detailed guidance and a quick GUI about how to navigate the program, it will make it much simpler for older people to do the work. Overall, the project proposal was brought to attention by the tutors with a minimum of literal, and I hope this project proposal should be followed.
